The Saints

Certain peopole have lived a life that can be considered so pleasing to God that they have earned the title of Saint. This site presents only three of such people, though there is a lifetime of Saints to learn from. The Saints are our eternal and universal family in God, and through each other, we come to reach the great reward, which is life in God.
